✦ How It Started

From Lucknow,
With Love

Navyra Jewellers started in Lucknow with a simple belief — every woman deserves jewellery that is beautiful, genuine, and affordable. We handpick each piece for its quality, design, and craftsmanship.

Our collection focuses on 925 sterling silver — the perfect balance of purity and durability. From delicate pendants to bold statement necklaces, every piece in our collection is chosen with care.

Today, we serve hundreds of happy customers across India, delivering the same quality and trust that we started with.
